\section{Experiments}
All models are trained on the train/test split provided by the UCI HAR dataset.
We performed various experiments to investigate the effects of our corruption framework on the
models trained on the dataset.
% \subsection{Parameter Sweep}
To characterize how each corruption type affects performance, we sweep each type across its
severity levels and report accuracy and Macro-$F_1$ as a function of severity. We evaluate all four
models under two granularity conditions: accelerometer modality only, and gyroscope modality only.
This is done to study the effect of the corruption on different modalities to observe the more
sensitive modality to corruption.
